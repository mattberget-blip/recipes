The best lasagna recipe ever!

About This Recipe:

This is the recipe of my favorite lasagna recipe to make in a pinch! If you have only 30 minutes to put something together for dinner, this is the one for you. It's easy for a family dinner during the week when time is limited!

Ingredient List:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Garlic
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Italian Seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Boil noodles and cool, then place noodles in casserole dish, lining the bottom completely
  2. Spead tomato sauce over noodles and sprinkle with cheese, garlic and italian seasoning
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until you fill up the dish
  4. Put the lasagna in the oven at 350F for 15 minutes or until browned
  5. Let cool for 5-10 minutes and enjoy!
