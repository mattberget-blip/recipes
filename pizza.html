The best pizza recipe ever!

About This Recipe:

This is the recipe of my favorite pizza when I was a kid! I used to make this all the time with my dad. It's perfect for a family night weekday and doesn't take long to put together for dinner.

Ingredient List:

  • Dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Instructions:

  1. Kneed our Dough
  2. Spead tomato sauce onto circular dough
  3. Add cheese on top of our tomato sauce
  4. Spead pepperoni onto our pizza
  5. Put the pizza in the oven at 350F for 30 minutes
  6. Let the pizza cool and enjoy!
