The best steak recipe ever!

About This Recipe:

This is the recipe of my favorite steak to make that doesn't take long. I make this when I need to make something elevated but short on time. It's perfect to put together in short time but look like you spend hours slaving over it!

Ingredient List:

  • Ribeye or NY steak
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic

Instructions:

  1. Put 3 TBSP butter and 1 TBSP oil in pan and heat to melt butter
  2. Place rosemary and garlic in simmering butter for 30 seconds
  3. Add steak to hot pan and cook for 7 minutes each side
  4. As it cooks, spoon the melted butter and ingredients over the steak to evenly coat
  5. After 7 minutes each side, place steak on cutting board to rest for 5-10 minutes
  6. Servce with your favorite side and enjoy!
